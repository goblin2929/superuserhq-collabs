## Key Design Decisions (discuss with Yaohong)

1. **Breakout group structure** — Do we split by team (sales / marketing / TDD) or mix them? By team = more relevant exercises. Mixed = cross-pollination.
2. **Depth vs breadth** — Do we show MCP integrations (Google Drive, CRM, etc.) or keep it focused on agents + skills only? Risk of overloading in 3 hours.
3. **Takeaway format** — Do participants walk out with (a) one working skill their team shares, or (b) a personal workflow they each built? Option (a) is harder but stickier.
4. **Follow-up** — Is this a one-shot or will there be follow-up support? Changes how ambitious we go.
